About Us

Help the poor and needy people...

Welcome to Helping Hands, a small group of passionate students dedicated to creating sustainable change. In these trying times, when the world is facing extremes of nature and the madness of men, we strive to provide the healing touch humanity so desperately needs today. We firmly believe that a good deed; however small it may be, can go a long way and bring joy to others. So many unfortunate souls struggle for necessities every day and we must extend our Helping Hands to them.

We are a group of like-minded people who understands the world needs all the helping hands it can get. We realise that disparity among the citizens of our beautiful country cannot be wished away. We are the people who step up, as per our capacity, to help in any way we can. We, along with our family and friends wish to be the change that we want to see.

Why to choose us

Our goal is to help the poor people...

We are a non-profit organisation committed to bringing some relief to the helpless and needy. We are small but our dreams are big. Our organisation has been reaching out and distributing food to one hundred people for the last two years in and around Bardhaman locality, during pandemic. With our limited resources, we have been trying to maximise our impact and this is where we need your help. Join us in any capacity you can and pledge your support to the cause of helping others.
